What is Zero Waste?

The goal of the zero waste movement is to encourage individuals to evaluate their lifestyles and reflect on how consumerist habits are detrimental to the health of the earth. The concept of zero waste goes beyond just recycling and composting. It is built upon the ideas of community, sustainability, ethics, and transparency. You too can participate by being mindful of the products that you consume and the of waste that you contribute to the planet every day, and WasteLess can help.

The WasteLess App

Aiding the zero waste community in learning, sharing, and saving the earth!

Each one of our features aids users to reduce their waste in a unique way, attempting to ease the transition into the zero waste lifestyle.

Reduce

Our app encourages users to reduce the amount of waste they produce by providing the means to track progress over time, set goals, and receive reminders.

Explore

Read posts from experienced and passionate zero wasters to learn tips and tricks on how to reduce your impact.

Equip

Browse products from online zero waste shops that offer package-free and sustainable solutions to help you reduce your dependency on disposable consumer goods.

Connect

Get involved in the zero waste community by sharing your progress and connecting with others.

The Development Process

  • User Research

    From the beginning, we worked with our target users to learn how we could build an app that would actually benefit the zero waste community. We conducted user testing along the way and incorporated the feedback from zero wasters into our design.

  • Web Scraping

    To connect our users to the existing zero waste community, we scraped information from over 13 blogs and 8 stores, generating over 2,000 blog posts and products that users can access from within the app.

  • Firebase

    We use Firebase as our backend to store all of the data we collected. Additionally, we use Firebase user authentication which makes it easy for us to manage our users and ensures proper security of user accounts.

  • React Native

    The app was built using React Native, a popular framework for building native mobile apps using JavaScript and React.
